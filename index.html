About the City

Bhubaneswar, the capital of Odisha, is a city where tradition meets modernity. Known as the “Temple City of India”, it houses ancient temples, cultural heritage, and fast-developing infrastructure.

Culture

Rich festivals, Odissi dance, and temple rituals.

Education

Home to IIT, AIIMS, and leading universities.

Tourism

Lingaraj Temple, Nandankanan Zoo, Khandagiri & Udayagiri.
