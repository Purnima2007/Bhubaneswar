About Bhubaneswar

The Temple City of India

General Information

Bhubaneswar, the capital of Odisha, is a historic and modern hub of temples, culture, and growth.

History

Known as the land of temples, Bhubaneswar has a legacy of 2000 years, from the Kalinga Empire to Ashoka's transformation.

Geography

Surrounded by rivers, fertile land, and green hills, the city has a tropical climate and scenic beauty.
